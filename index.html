About Us

At Beanie, we are passionate about coffee. We source our beans from the finest farms around the world and roast them to perfection. Our baristas are skilled in crafting the perfect cup of coffee just for you.

Our Mission

Our mission is to turn simple coffee moments into special memories. From carefully selecting the finest beans to serving each cup with passion, we believe coffee is not just a drink — it’s a lifestyle that brings people together.
